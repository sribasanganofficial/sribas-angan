About Us

Sribas Angan Bhakti Vriksha is a spiritual initiative focused on cultivating bhakti yoga, Vedic wisdom, and community transformation through devotional service and personal growth.

What is Bhakti Vriksha?

Bhakti Vriksha, the Tree of Devotion, represents a small group program aimed at helping individuals grow spiritually through regular study, chanting, and association.
